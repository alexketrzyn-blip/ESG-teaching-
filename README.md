# README – Training Course for Upholstered Furniture Company

##  Overview
This course was designed for an **employee of the purchasing department** in a company producing upholstered furniture.  
The repository contains instructional materials and practical exercises tailored to professional communication and procurement tasks.

---

##  Repository Contents
- **Empathy Map** – to better understand the employee’s needs, motivations, and challenges.  
- **Persona Description** – a detailed profile of the target learner.  
- **Course Program** – structured learning objectives and modules.  
- **Lesson Example** – focused on writing an email inquiry about product certificates.  
- **Learner’s Results Analysis** – evaluation of performance and progress during the course.  

---

##  Purpose
The course aims to:  
- Strengthen communication skills in procurement processes.  
- Provide practical tools for writing professional emails.  
- Support learning through empathy-based design and persona-driven training.  
- Deliver measurable outcomes through analysis of learner performance.  

---

##  Key Takeaway
This repository demonstrates the integration of **instructional design, technical writing, and practical training methods** for employees in the furniture manufacturing sector.
